Here at XXXXX, henceforth referred to as the Operator, we uphold the Users' right to decide on the sharing of their information. We want to inform you that we utilize cookie technology and geolocation on our Website.

Cookies are small pieces of text data sent by a web server and stored on the User's device. By default, cookies are designed to be read only by the server that created them. Facilitating the User's Website experience through session maintenance (after logging in), eliminating the need for the User to re-enter login credentials on each page. Customizing Website content to align with User preferences and optimizing Website usage. These files allow recognition of the User's device and ensure proper display of the Website tailored to individual needs. Gathering anonymous aggregate statistical data to enhance understanding of User behavior on the Website, thereby improving the Website's structure and content in line with User expectations. Our Website employs two primary types of cookies: session cookies and persistent cookies. Session cookies are temporary files stored on the User's device until they log out, leave the Website, or close their web browser. Persistent cookies remain on the User's device for a specified period or until the User deletes them.
